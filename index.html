Blazon Proportions Template

Use these templates to quickly test out how your blazon may look across different shield shapes.

  • Featuring ten basic geometric shapes, these templates can give you insight into a broad pool of potential emblazons.
  • Each shield shape features optical center marks and a dot grid equally divisible into thirds or fourths for fast measurements of ordinaries and other geometric elements.
  • Included are pages for 1-inch thumbnail sketches, or more detailed 2-inch drawings.
  • Availalbe in PDF and PNG filetypes. Print them out or import them into your favorite digital drawing app!
  • Rendered in a cyan-like color, these pages are perfect for easily removing the guidelines when scanning.
  • The thumbnails template is also available in a charcoal outlined version, to help save you additional time.
